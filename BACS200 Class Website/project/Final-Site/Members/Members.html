Our members

Below is a list of our organization's thirteen members.

Xemnas - I

Nickname - The Superior In-Between
Attribute - Nothingness
Weapon – Ethereal Blades: Interdiction
Previous Self -Xehanort
Bio – Xemnas is the leader and first official member of Organization XII. He is the most powerful among us, even possessing the ability to turn Nobodies into lesser versions. We follow and obey him on our mission to summon Kingdom Hearts.

Xigbar - II

Nickname - The Freeshooter
Attribute - Space
Weapon – Arrowguns: Sharpshooters
Previous Self - Braig
Bio – Braig is the second member to join Organization XII. His use of precision weapons and the attribute of space make him a deadly member of the Organization.

Xaldin - III

Nickname - The Whirlwind Lancer
Attribute - Wind
Weapon – 6 Lances: Lindworm
Previous Self - Dilan
Bio – Xaldin is the third member to join Organization XII. He controls the attribute wind, which when combined with the use of his 6 Lindworm lances, creates a powerful force not to be reckoned with.

Vexen - IV

Nickname - The Chilly Academic
Attribute - Ice
Weapon – Shield: Frozen Pride
Previous Self - Even
Bio – Vexen is the fourth member to join Organization XII. His discipline of the attribute ice, and his even colder demeanor, make him a valuable asset to the Organization.

Lexaeus - V

Nickname - The Taciturn Stalwart
Attribute - Earth
Weapon – Axe Sword: Skysplitter
Previous Self - Aeleus
Bio – It's only fitting that Lexaeus controls the attribute Earth. Our Fifth member may be the slowest of us, but when he combines his element with the weapon Skysplitter it makes him the hardest hitting member in the Organization.

Zexion - VI

Nickname - The Cloaked Schemer
Attribute - Illusion
Weapon – Lexicon: Book of Retribution
Previous Self - Ienzo
Bio – Our sixth member Zexion may be one of the youngest members, but at times his wisdom exceeds that of even Xenmas himself. He uses his lack of emotion and the power of illusion to mercilessly fool his opponents.

Saix - VII

Nickname - The Luna Diviner
Attribute - Moon
Weapon – Claymore: Lunatic
Previous Self - Isa
Bio – The Seventh member Saix appears calm and collected, all the while holding back a formidable fury powered by the moon. He may be the seventh member, but he is considered one of the leaders among the organization.

Axel - VIII

Nickname - The Flurry of Dancing Flames
Attribute - Fire
Weapon – Duel Chakrams: Eternal Flames
Previous Self - Lea
Bio – As one of our more light hearted members, the eighth member Axel comes off as childish; however, Axel's skill with the Eternal Flames and his dedication to the Organization make him a valuable asset to our mission.

Demyx - IX

Nickname - The Melodious Nocturne
Attribute - Water
Weapon – Sitar: Arpeggio
Previous Self - Unknown
Bio – Our ninth member Demyx is another one of our members who seems to lack the seriousness of others. But dont be fooled, his control over water paired with his sitar, Arpeggio make him a formidable opponent.

Luxord - X

Nickname - The Gambler of Fate
Attribute - Time
Weapon – Cards: Fair Game
Previous Self - Unknown
Bio – Like Saix, our tenth member Luxord is looked at as a leader amongst the organization. He forces his opponents to play his games, and for those who don't learn how to play by his rules, the outcome can have dire consequences.

Marluxia - XI

Nickname - The Graceful Assassin
Attribute - Flower
Weapon – Scythe: Graceful Dahlia
Previous Self - Unknown
Bio – Our eleventh member Marluxia may appear unthreatening on the surface, but his swift and elegent attacks with Graceful Dahlia make him one of the more dangerous members in the Organization.

Larxene - XII

Nickname - The Savage Nymph
Attribute - Lightning
Weapon – Knives: Foudre
Previous Self – Unknown
Bio – Larxene, our twelveth member, is one of the smaller in the Organization. But dont let her size fool you, Larxene's attacks with Foudre are lightning quick and powerful enough to take down any foe she may face.

Roxas - XIII

Nickname - The Key of Destiny
Attribute - Light
Weapon – Duel Keyblades: Oathkeeper and Oblivion
Previous Self – Sora
Bio – Although our thirteenth and last official member to join, Roxas is vital to our success of summoning Kingdom Hearts. His Keyblade harvests the hearts from Heartless, and every Strike of his weapon brings Kingdom Hearts ever closer to our grasp.

Xion*

Nickname - None
Attribute - Light
Weapon – Keyblade: Kingdom Key
Previous Self – Unknown
Bio – Xion is currently not a member of Organization XIII; however we hope to soon add her to our Ranks, as she, like Roxas, possesses the Keyblade and all of its abilities.

*The would be fourteenth member Xion is "going through a trial run" within our oganization, and is currently not considered a full member.
