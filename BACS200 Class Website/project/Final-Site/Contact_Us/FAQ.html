Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How did you all create your name when you joined the Organization?

A: Its actually really simple. We just add an X to our name, then we rearrange the letters.


Q: Why do you all wear black robes?

A: Our black capes not only give us a uniform, fashnionable appearance, but they also protect us from the darkness when we travel between worlds.


Q: Why do you all use the weapons that you use?

A: We use the weapons that you see us with because they are what we used before we became nobodies.


Q: Why is your color scheme only black and white?

A: Being that we are "nothing", it makes sense that we would choose those colors.
